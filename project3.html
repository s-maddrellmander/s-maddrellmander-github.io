Rare Decay Measurements in Particle Physics

During my PhD at the University of Bristol and CERN, I focused on measuring extremely rare decays in particle physics. This research, published in Nature Physics, contributed to our understanding of fundamental particle interactions and tested the limits of the Standard Model.

Key Achievements

  • Developed advanced statistical analysis techniques for rare event detection
  • Implemented machine learning approaches for particle track reconstruction
  • Contributed to a publication in Nature Physics on lepton universality tests
  • Presented findings at international conferences, including the Lake Louise Winter Institute
