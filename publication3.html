GPS++: Reviving the Art of Message Passing for Molecular Property Prediction

TMLR 2023

This paper introduces GPS++, an advanced Graph Neural Network model that combines the strengths of message passing neural networks and transformers for improved molecular property prediction. We demonstrate state-of-the-art performance on several benchmark datasets, including the OGB-LSC PCQM4Mv2 challenge.

Key Contributions

  • Novel hybrid architecture combining GNNs and Transformers
  • Improved message passing scheme for capturing long-range interactions in molecules
  • Extensive ablation studies demonstrating the effectiveness of each component
  • State-of-the-art results on multiple molecular property prediction benchmarks
